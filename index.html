Which Teams are the Most Active on Offense and Defense?

Understanding the Chart

This grid visualizes NBA teams based on their offensive and defensive "activity" during the season. It helps to quickly see which teams are more active on each end of the court.

  • Offensive Activity (X-axis): This measures a team's offensive movement, with higher scores reflecting more passing and player movement. Teams on the right feature more possessions with a lot of movement and passing
  • Defensive Activity (Y-axis): This measures a team's defensive aggressiveness, with higher scores indicating more aggressive tactics like pick-and-roll blitzes and a higher frequency of fouls. Teams at the top blitz/show and foul more.
  • Team Name Size: The size of each team's name is scaled to its number of playoff wins.
